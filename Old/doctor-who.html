Doctor Who (2005-2022)

Medium: Show
Rating: 8.5 (IMDB)

The Doctor's epic adventures across Space and Time in that famous blue box. It's the trip of a lifetime.

Meeting many companions along the way, the Doctor fights evil accross the universe.

This is the revival show that went on from 2005-2022. The original show started in 1963 and ran until 1989. The new show started in 2023 and is a contiunation of the 2005-2022 revival.

Description provided by the BBC and the community.

Trigger Warnings

  • Portrayal of racism
  • Body horror and human experimentation (Series 10)
  • Cheating


Negative Representations

Downplaying consent
In The Beast Below (S5E2), a character forces herself upon the Doctor, repeatedly kissing him whilst he clearly states he doesn't want it. In The Crimson Horror (S7E11), the Doctor kisses a someone without consent, she slaps him in retaliation.

Cheating
One character cheats on her partner throughout series 5 which is often played as a joke. There's a sort of forced love triangle that feels very uncomfortable.
