Purpose

The idea behind this website is to allow you to look at the potentially triggering content within pieces of media to make an informed decision as to whether it is right for you. We also provide descriptions of negative representations within media, such as racist portrayals etc.

This site is in development! See the GitHub page's readme to see progress our and roadmap :3
